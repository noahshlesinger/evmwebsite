Welcome to Emerald Valley Multisport

Who are we?

We are a community-driven triathlon club. Our mission is to tie together the loose strands of the triathlon community here in the Willamette Valley to create a supportive and motivated group of endurance athletes.

Become a Member!

General Membership

This is open to all EVM members. Whether you're brand new to triathlon or a seasoned vet , this is the place for you. You'll get to be a part of a growing triathlon community who will support and encourage your own journey as they pursue their own. Why become a member of this awesome club? You'll receive updated meet-up schedules, access to sponsor discounts, team orders and any training camps that we put on. You can also receive personallized training plans and coaching at a disocunted rate from one our coaches. (Note: You don't need to hire one of our coaches in order to be a member.)

Elite Membership

This is for the more competitive athlete with aspirations of winning their age group, earning a spot at a world championship, and for anyone who already has or is working toward achieving their elite license. This includes everything that the general membership offers as well.
