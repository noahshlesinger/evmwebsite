About Us

We are a community-driven triathlon club. Our mission is to tie together the loose strands of the triathlon community here in the Willamette Valley to create a supportive and motivated group of endurance athletes.

Become a Member!

General Membership

This is open to all EVM members. Whether you're brand new to triathlon or a seasoned vet , this is the place for you. You'll get to be a part of a growing triathlon community who will support and encourage your own journey as they pursue their own.

Elite Membership

This is for the more competitive athlete with aspirations of winning their age group, earning a spot at a world championship, and for anyone who already has or is working toward achieving their elite license.
