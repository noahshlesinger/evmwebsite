Local Meet-Ups

Reminder: These Meet-ups are open to everyone at all levels. There will likely be a variety in abalities, but don't let stop you from showing up and hopefully finding other athletes at or around your ability. The purpose of the Meet Up is simply to establish a time and place to link up and are ultimately run by the members in attendance unless otherwise stated below. From there, you can decide if you want to join in on a hard workout or keep it aerobic. Hope to see you there!

  • Monday Bike Ride (No-Drop)

    Time: 1pm

    Location: @ Cycle Way of Life (Alder st Location)

    Description: ~26 miles (Hilly-Fox Hollow Climb)

  • Wednesday Bike Ride (No-Drop)

    Time: 6:00pm

    Location: Way of Life (Charnelton St. Location)

    Description: Social Ride ~27 miles

  • Friday Aerobic Run

    Time: 4:30pm

    Location: South Eugene Track Parking Lot

    Description: Aerobic Mileage Run. 5-7 miles. We'll start out together than split up depending on pace.

  • Saturday "Group" Swim

    Time: 10am

    Location: UO Rec Pool

    Description: For anyone joining this swim, it's an OYO (On Your Own) swim which means there won't be a coach giving out workouts. Please check UO Rec website for any closures. (like 4/26)
