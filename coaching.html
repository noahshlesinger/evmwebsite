Noah Shlesinger

Professional Triathlete & USAT Certified Coach

Biography

Noah is a professional triathlete with 5 years of experience in the sport. He has competed in numerous 70.3 Ironmans, Olympic-distance, and sprint-distance events, securing several podiums and wins. Throughout his career, Noah has gained invaluable insights into training, performance optimization, and mental toughness, making him a great asset for beginners and professionals alike. Noah's most recent athlete completed their first olympic-distance triathlon in the spring of 2024, giving them the confidence to train for and complete the 70.3 Ironman in Salem, OR that same year beating their goal of sub 6 hours by 15 minutes!
